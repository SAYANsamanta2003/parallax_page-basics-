A parallax website is a design technique where background elements move at different speeds to create an illusion of depth, enhancing user engagement. As users scroll, foreground and background layers move independently, offering a dynamic visual experience. This design style is often employed to tell compelling stories or showcase products/services creatively. Parallax scrolling adds a sense of interactivity and aesthetics to web pages, making them more visually appealing. It's widely utilized in modern web design to captivate visitors, convey narratives, and provide a memorable browsing experience through its innovative use of motion and depth effects.
